Bees have never been afraid to change the world. I mean, what about Bee Columbus, Bee Ghandi, Bee-Jesus?

We at Save The Bees are passionate about bees. We care about the health and well bee-ing of all types of bee as we understand their importanace to society. We hope to spread the word of the plight of the bees and how everyone can pitch in to help our stripey friends. We have pages on what exactly is happening to the bees, and how you can help. We also have a page on the Bees Knees.
